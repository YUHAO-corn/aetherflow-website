Subscription Terms

All subscriptions will automatically renew at the end of your billing period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings.

By subscribing, you authorize us to charge your payment method at the then-current rates. If the payment method fails, we may attempt to charge any other payment method associated with your account.

Price changes may occur at any time with notice provided to subscribers. If you don't agree with the new price, you can cancel your subscription before the price change takes effect.

For more details, please review our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Refund Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by accessing your account settings. After cancellation, you'll continue to have access to the Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

If you downgrade from Pro to Free, any prompts exceeding the Free plan limit will be preserved but marked as read-only. You can view them but not edit or use them. They will become fully accessible again if you resubscribe to a Pro plan.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you're not satisfied with our service. Please see our Refund Policy for complete details.

Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?

Yes, you can switch between plans at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at your next billing cycle.
